## E-Commerce Sales & Revenue Analysis (Excel | Data Analytics Project)

### 📌 Project Summary
1. This project explores an E-commerce dataset using Microsoft Excel to evaluate overall business performance, purchasing patterns, and revenue trends.

2. The analysis combines Customer, Product, Sales, and Store datasets to generate meaningful business insights through systematic data cleaning, transformation, and aggregation techniques.

3. The primary goal of this project is to convert raw transactional data into valuable insights that can support revenue growth, customer engagement, and operational efficiency.

## 🎯 Business Objectives
1. Identify top-performing and underperforming products
2. Evaluate the contribution of customer loyalty segments to revenue
3. Compare purchasing behavior across genders
4. Examine quarterly and seasonal sales patterns
5. Analyze category-level and regional revenue performance
6. Assess the contribution of different store types to total sales

## 🛠️ Tools & Techniques
Tool Used

1. Microsoft Excel
2. Techniques Applied

- Data Cleaning and Formatting
- Pivot Tables and Pivot Charts
- Calculated Fields and Derived Metrics
- Conditional Analysis
- Sorting, Filtering, and Data Grouping
- Revenue and Trend Analysis
## 📂 Dataset Overview
The dataset is composed of four interconnected tables:

- Customer – Contains demographic information and loyalty program details
- Product – Includes category, brand, and inventory-related attributes
- Sales – Contains transactional revenue records
- Store – Includes store locations and sales channels
- The Sales table acts as the primary fact table, linking customer, product, and store information to enable comprehensive analysis.

## 📊 Key Analytical Insights
### 1. Category-wise Revenue Insights
- Sports is the highest revenue-generating category across most regions.
- Electronics consistently contributes high sales and is one of the strongest-performing product categories.
- Beauty and Clothing generate moderate revenue with noticeable year-to-year fluctuations.
- Home products show comparatively lower revenue, indicating an opportunity for improvement.
- Revenue patterns differ by region, suggesting that customer preferences vary geographically.
### 2. Year-wise Revenue Insights
- Total revenue increased significantly from 2023 to 2024, indicating strong business growth.
- Revenue in 2025 remains high but appears slightly lower than the 2024 peak.
- The East and North regions contribute the largest share of revenue.
- The South and West regions contribute comparatively less, suggesting scope for market expansion.
### 3. Regional Performance Insights
- The East region is the top-performing market in terms of sales.
- The North region also delivers strong and consistent revenue.
- The South and West regions show lower performance, highlighting potential areas for targeted marketing and promotional campaigns.
### 4. Forecasting Insights
- Monthly sales generally remain between 80,000 and 100,000, indicating stable demand.
- There are periodic sales spikes, likely driven by seasonal promotions or festive periods.
- The forecast trendline suggests that future sales are expected to remain stable if current business conditions continue.
- A sharp drop toward the end of the timeline may indicate seasonal variation or incomplete data.

## 🚀 Conclusion
This project highlights the ability to transform raw transactional data into clear and actionable business insights using Excel.

The dashboard indicates that the business experienced strong revenue growth between 2023 and 2024, with Sports and Electronics leading sales performance. The East and North regions contribute the highest revenue, while South and West offer opportunities for growth. Forecasting suggests stable future sales, making this a good time to strengthen marketing strategies and optimize inventory planning for sustained business growth.
